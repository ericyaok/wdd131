Accra, Ghana

Ghana, located on West Africa's Gulf of Guinea, is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and vibrant economy. With Accra as its capital, Ghana gained independence from British colonial rule in 1957, becoming the first sub-Saharan African nation to do so. The country is celebrated for its political stability and commitment to democracy.
